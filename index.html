VEE NETS

We manufactre and sell all kinds of Nylon Monofilament, Nylon Multifilament and Nylon Copolymer fishing nets and also customised fishnets as per the customer's need. We assure to provide fishermen with good quality and cost effective fishnets.

Scroll down
quality Our fishnet is fabricated with high quality twine to enhance the durability yet soft to ensure good catch, manufactured by using latest technology driven Japan made Machines.

Vee nets was started by two brothers in 2012 Mr.Rajendran and Mr.Leon as partners who are traditional fishermen of a coastal village, Rajakkamangalam Thurai of southern tip of Peninsular India located near Kanyakumari, after a stint as fishing vessel captions in Saudi Arabia, became entrepreneurs, with a missionary zeal to supply quality fishnets to fellow fisher folks to augment them with best possible tools to harvest fishes spread in vast sea, lakes and rivers.
